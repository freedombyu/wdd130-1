Audience

Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts seeking exhilarating experiences in the great outdoors. They typically fall within the age range of 18 to 45, encompassing both young adventurers and active adults. Financially, they are middle-income individuals or families willing to invest in memorable outdoor adventures.

Their Life (or Business) Situation

Our customers have a passion for adventure and are often outdoor enthusiasts. They seek to break away from their routine lives and immerse themselves in nature. They are adventurous spirits, open to new challenges and experiences, and value quality time spent with friends and family.

What They Want

Our audience craves thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences that provide a respite from their daily grind. They want to create lasting memories, enjoy beautiful natural landscapes, and relish the thrill of conquering river rapids. They also seek convenience and ease when booking their trips.

Their Needs That Aren't Being Met

While there are other rafting companies, our audience is looking for a combination of safety, expertise, and exceptional customer service that truly sets us apart. They want a seamless booking process and access to comprehensive information about their rafting adventure.

How They Will Access Our Site

Our audience predominantly accesses websites through mobile devices and laptops. They appreciate the convenience of browsing and booking adventures on the go, as they are often planning trips from different locations and want information readily available at their fingertips.
